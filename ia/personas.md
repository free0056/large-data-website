##PERSONA 2

### Fredrika Baggins, 55, Female, Small business owner

Fredrika is a hard working individual with some experience with mobile devices but somewhat limited in her capacity to navigate complicated websites. She is an important target persona since she represents a portion of the public that enjoys science and learning but is frustrated with gimmicks or over complicated web pages.

Fredrika wants to expand her knowledge in ares of science and always tries to find new sources of information that can feed her needs.

Fredrika has some knowledge of handheld media and has a pc at home she uses for email and the like and is familiar with some simple phone apps and games.
Likes passing the time with simple flash games or touch screen applications, dislikes complicated sites or useless applications, small hard to read fonts, and small buttons.
  
Things that would help her navigate the site would be large clear buttons and links, and big headings and interesting layouts that would help the content flow with out being too boring.
